i'm emily

But feel free to call me Emi. In my childhood, I was actually called Antony but things change. What hasn't changed is my love for computers. My parents sacrified most of their savings so I could have a PC when I was three. Now, my computer is a crucial character in my life, connecting me with people around the world and enabling me to pursue a growing career in software development.
My upbringing has made me understand the importance of being introduced to interests early on and so my dream is that coding can be readily accessible to everyone. I utilize my love of design in order to develop tools and write curriculum that help people understand complex concepts in simple manners. My work has a theme of being playful, with a strong emphasis on discoverability. It keeps learners engaged, piquing both curiosity and creativity.
When I'm not coding, I'm playing League of Legends with my beloved cousin or downing loads of boba with my bestie Ysabelle. I also can't stop moving. I often drive hundreds of miles to visit my siblings and I'm often running thousands more. To compensate for the damage to my feet, my everyday shoes are either a pair of fast Heelys or some comfy Crocs. Below, you'll find my bucket list. It's basically my life's version of a resume. I seek new experiences so that I have a constant supply of stories to inspire others with. Only by seeing and doing can I grow and so I aim to see and do as much as possible.
