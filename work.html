my work

When I was 3, my first generation immigrant parents spent most of their savings building me a computer, hoping that it would lead me to a bright future. Through their sacrifices, I've been able to pursue my love of engineering and design. I now spend my time teaching workshops, volunteering at schools, and designing tools to help others learn to code. My work reflects my dream - a dream of a world where anyone can code their way to a bright future.
